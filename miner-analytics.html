Sector Context

Today's themes in narrative form

The miner-to-HPC pivot remains the dominant 2026 theme, with the gap between hyperscaler-deal winners and laggards continuing to widen. WULF, HUT, IREN, CORZ and CIFR are now functioning more like AI infrastructure landlords than mining companies. Today's macro backdrop is unfriendly: Bitcoin is testing support, Fed and ECB decisions land this week, and the Iran war keeps oil and shipping costs elevated. Power and grid bottlenecks remain the biggest constraint on near-term capacity additions. The five 1 GW data centers expected online in 2026 will see additional 11 GW of announced projects sitting idle without grid connections.

Within the small-cap names, SLNH and MIGI are reshaping under new pressure. SLNH is digesting the 26.51M share registration overhang while operationally executing on Briscoe and Kati. MIGI is mid-rebrand to Big Digital Energy with a new board and exec team, and the BGDE ticker takes effect 30 April. Both still trade as turnaround stories rather than peer-group standouts.
